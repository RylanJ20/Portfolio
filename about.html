About

Hi! I'm Rylan and I'm an IT Support Specialist with Twenty2 Technologies LLC.

Professional Interests

I graduated from the University of Louisville in 2022 with a BSBA in Computer Information Systems with tracks in Business Process Management, Information Security, and Web Development. I am currently located in the San Francisco Bay Area of California!

My dream job is to work at a major gaming company. After being introduced to game engines and developing games, whenever I play a game, I can only think about what it took to develop certain aspects of it. After playing and thinking like that for a few years, it made me want to create my own games and be able to make important decisions on them as well.

I look forward to a long career in tech where I can continue to learn as much as possible and helping a company however I can with the skills that I have now and that I will gain along the way. My long term goal is to have a high up role a major game company where I can work with game developers and help create fun games for people to play! I love to make ideas come to life and make online experiences more fun overall for everyone!

Casual Interests

I put a lot of time trying to improve and learned a lot about the Esports community as a whole. Nowadays, I enjoy developing games almost as much as I do playing them.

Because of this I found my own teams to support and I ended up choosing the Suns and the Vikings for Basketball and Football respectively.
