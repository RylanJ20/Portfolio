CIS Development Project

The CIS Development Project was a two-semester long project that I worked on in college. I was assigned a team of 3 other people and we all worked together on the project. The first semester was built around project management and we initiated and planned the project. The second semester was built around the actual development of the project. The project was a mentorship site for the CIS Department at U of L, and you can find the finished product by clicking here.
